## Statement
> Player i's total payoff is the discounted sum where (i) δ is a common discount factor and δ<1, and (ii) v_i is a bounded one-period valuation function: |v_i(θ_i,a)| ≤ C < ∞ for all θ_i and a.

## Role in Model
拟线性支付保证转移可加;有界 valuation 与 δ<1 保证无限期贴现和收敛、最大值可取。
